Our Vision

Archistructure Group Company is an Architectural and Construction Company located in Freetown, Sierra Leone, West Africa that came into existence since 2018. Is a company that focuses on architectural drawings, interior and exterior decorations and implantation of both residential and commercial building projects.

The company was setup and headed by Rashid Harun Turay and Musa Saidu Mansaray, both architectural graduates from Limkokwing University of creative Technology with over 5 years of working experience in the construction industry.
